BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATION BCA Overview PROGRAMME EDUCATIONAL OBJECTIVES : This program aims to achieve the following objectives: To equip the students to meet the requirement of the Corporate world and Industry standard. To engage in professional development and to pursue post-graduate education in the fields of Information Technology and Computer Applications To provide the students about computing principles and business practices in software solutions, outsourcing services, public and private sectors PROGRAM OUTCOMES (PO): BCA graduates should be able to: Understand the concepts of key areas in computer science. Analyze and apply the latest technologies to solve problems in the areas of computer applications. Analyze and synthesis computing systems through quantitative and qualitative techniques Apply technical and professional skills to excel in business. Communicate effectively in both verbal and written form. Develop practical skills to provide solutions to industry, society and business. BCA:- 3 years degree in the field of Computer application Eligibility: Student should be from math’s side or commercial math’s in intermediate with 50% minimum marks. TOTAL NUMBER OF SEATS: 120
